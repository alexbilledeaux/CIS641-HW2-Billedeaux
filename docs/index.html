About the Project

The PythonGameGuys are working on a new roguelike video game, where the player explores procedurally-generated dungeons, battles monsters, and collects loot. Not only will the project provide hands-on experience with a new programming language for the team (Python), but it will also serve as a comprehensive education in requirement specification. By the time the semester is finished, we will have practical experience in creating a Software Requirements Specification and CRC cards.
